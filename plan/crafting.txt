We should have a comprehensive crafting system. Also cooking. So that we can
make lasagna.

There are several ways to learn to make things.
- Intrinsic knowledge:
    Learn recipes just by levelling skill enough, can see recipes a level
    before using them.
    Good for cooking.
- Learn from master:
    Talking to the right NPCs, completing the right quests, or reading the
    right books can give recipes.
    Good for magic.
- Experiment:
    Throw ingredients together and hope for the best.
    Good for potion-making.
